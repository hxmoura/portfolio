   ### Search and filter products

Search for the product you want using the search bar or select price and brand filters to refine the results. This feature allows you to quickly find items that match your preferences and saves your search history, making browsing more efficient and convenient.

### View information about each product

View complete details for each product, including technical specifications, images, user reviews, and descriptions. This feature allows visitors to make informed decisions before adding an item to their cart, offering a more complete and intuitive browsing experience.

<ImageView imageUrl="/casa-moura02.png" />

</Section>

<Section className="space-y-7">
### Add products to the cart

Manage your products in the shopping cart conveniently by adding, removing, or changing the quantity of each item. Review the detailed order summary, including the final price and shipping calculation.

<ImageView imageUrl="/casa-moura03.png" />

</Section>

<Section className="space-y-7">
### Complete your order

Complete your purchase quickly and securely by creating an account or using an existing one, entering the shipping address, and providing payment details integrated with Mercado Pago. The entire process takes place within the website, without the need for redirection, ensuring convenience and security for the user.
